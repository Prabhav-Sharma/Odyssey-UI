Card

Cards are surfaces that display content and actions on a single topic.

Card with overlay-text

To use Card with text-overlay, copy & paste the below code. 

<div class="card-midtext">
<div class=img-container>
<img class="prod-img" src="/CSS/Components/assets/1.jpg"></div> 
 <p class="overlay-text">MEN</p>
 </div>
